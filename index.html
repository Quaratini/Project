Who We Are

Welcome to Quarantini Hop-Shop. We are a small local PNW brewery located in the heart of Seattle. We brew and carry beers of all backgrounds. We started off brewing in our parents garages. What was originally just a fun off to the side hobby, then turned into a local well known beer brand. We then figured since we've been doing this for so long and had so many request from friends and family, that we'd go into business.
